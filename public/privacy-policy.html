Home / Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you submit voluntarily through our consultation forms, contact queries, and newsletters. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and specific building/design preferences.

2. How We Use Information

We use your contact details to respond to estimate inquiries, set up layout consult appointments, and send design newsletters if subscribed. We do not sell or lease your customer details to third-party databases.

3. Data Security

We use standard security mechanisms to store and protect your inquiries in our MongoDB backend. However, no transmission over the web can be guaranteed 100% secure.

4. Cookies

We use subtle analytical cookies to analyze visitor statistics and page load times. This helps us optimize site performance for mobile and desktop screens.
